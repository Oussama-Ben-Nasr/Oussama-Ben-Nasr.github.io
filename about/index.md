# About me

I'm a software developer passionate about clean code, functional programming, and building things that last. I write about what I learn — from diving deep into a new language to rethinking something I thought I already understood.

## Why this blog

I started writing because explaining something forces you to understand it properly. Every post here started as a note to myself — something that tripped me up, something that clicked, or something I wanted to remember.

## What I work with

My current stack revolves around **Scala** and the JVM ecosystem for backend work, **JavaScript** for the web, and whatever tool is best for the problem at hand. I care a lot about type systems, immutability, and making the right thing easy.

## Get in touch

Find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/Oussama-Ben-Nasr) — pull requests and conversations welcome.
